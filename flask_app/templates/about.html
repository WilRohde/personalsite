First thing's first...

Where has this guy been for the past five years? It's a question I get a lot, and it's a valid concern. If this guy is any good as a developer, what's he been doing? So let me explain.


Five years ago I was a contract developer at a company near Pittsburgh. My project was cancelled. I liked the project and it was a big disappointment, but contract work is contract work, and companies change direction all the time. It had happened before. I felt pretty confident about finding my next project.


Instead I found myself in the hospital for a week, and I was out of work for almost two years. It was a bad time. So I've spent the last few years getting back on my feet medically. I've worked a lot of odd jobs in the meantime to pay the bills.


What I hope this site shows is that I'm still a serious programmer. I would argue I'm actually better now than I was a few years ago.
